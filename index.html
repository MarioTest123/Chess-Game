Chess with Cameron

Learn every hero, then play a real game of chess. The board only lets you make legal moves — so you can’t go wrong while you learn!

1 · Meet your heroes

Tap a piece to see where it can go. Gold coins are squares you can move to; a red ring means you can capture there.

How you win: trap the other team’s King so it has nowhere safe to go. That’s called checkmate! You never actually grab the King — you just leave it no escape.

2 · Start the game

Against the computer you’re the Red team at the bottom, and you go first. Drag a hero with your finger, or tap it then tap where to go.
